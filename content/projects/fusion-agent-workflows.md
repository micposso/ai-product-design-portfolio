# Fusion Agent Workflows For Product Design

## Summary

An internal initiative at JPMorgan Chase focused on designing agentic workflows that support product design, content operations, prototyping, and AI-assisted software delivery inside the Fusion AI platform.

## Context

This work sits at the intersection of product design, front end systems, and enterprise AI. The goal is to create practical agent workflows that help teams move faster while still respecting regulatory constraints, accessibility requirements, and enterprise governance.

## What I Built

- Designed agent workflows to support research synthesis, content generation, testing, and design iteration.
- Built front end prototypes that demonstrate how conversational interfaces and agent experiences can fit into enterprise product workflows.
- Connected LLM-powered interactions with structured design and content pipelines.
- Explored patterns for AI-assisted collaboration between product, design, engineering, and content teams.

## Stack

Agentic AI systems, LLM workflows, React, Next.js, TypeScript, structured content pipelines, enterprise design systems

## Outcome

The project helped shape how agent-driven experiences can support product design and delivery in a large enterprise environment. It also informed broader thinking around AI-native interfaces and design systems inside Fusion AI.
