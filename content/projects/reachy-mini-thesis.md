# Embodied AI Language Learning Thesis With Reachy Mini

## Summary

A master's thesis in Information Design and Technology at SUNY Polytechnic Institute exploring how Reachy Mini can be used as an embodied AI interface for language learning and conversational interaction.

## Context

The research explores whether a physical AI presence can create a more engaging language-learning experience than a screen-only assistant. The thesis combines human-centered AI, conversational interfaces, multimodal interaction, and educational design.

## What I Built

- Designed the thesis concept around embodied AI for language learning.
- Used Reachy Mini as the physical interaction layer for the research.
- Explored conversational patterns that combine spoken interaction, physical embodiment, and guided educational flows.
- Investigated how embodied AI can affect trust, attention, and engagement in learning environments.

## Stack

Reachy Mini, conversational AI, embodied interaction design, language-learning workflows, human-centered AI research

## Outcome

The thesis serves as a research and prototype effort connecting embodied AI with language learning. It extends my broader interest in conversational systems, voice interaction, and human-centered AI experiences.
