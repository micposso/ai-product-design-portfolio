# AgentsOnly Trust Verification System

## Summary

AgentsOnly.io is a concept for an agent trust verification system that helps people evaluate whether AI agents are safe, transparent, and reliable enough to use.

## Context

As AI agents become more autonomous, trust becomes a product problem as much as a technical one. This project explores how to make agent verification more legible by exposing signals around behavior, permissions, provenance, and reliability.

## What I Built

- Defined the product concept for verifying agent trust and credibility.
- Explored ways to surface trust signals such as provenance, permissions, traceability, and safety posture.
- Considered how trust verification could be presented in a simple interface for users and teams evaluating AI agents.
- Positioned the project as part of my broader interest in AI safety, human-centered AI, and responsible agent design.

## Stack

Agent trust systems, AI safety concepts, verification workflows, product design, human-centered AI

## Outcome

AgentsOnly.io represents a product direction focused on trust infrastructure for autonomous systems. It connects strategic thinking around AI governance with practical UX and product framing.
